## Payment interfaces are divided into two categories

### CLICK payment interfaces

-   CLICK SuperApp mobile app

-   Payment Web service my.click.uz
-   USSD service

-   Telegram bot

### Merchant payment interfaces

-   Merchant website

-   Merchant mobile app

When merchant billing system meets requirements (implementation of **Prepare** and **Complete** requests described in SHOP API), merchant services can be available for payment via any payment interface.
